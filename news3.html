EduLang.Co 25th Graduation Ceremony

The Year 2021 EduLang.Co 25th Graduation Ceremony The Graduation Ceremony for F46 students was held on 21st December 2021(Tuesday) at 10:00 am. Unfortunately, due to the pandemic and for safety reasons, this year the graduation ceremony is still being held at online.

Even though the pandemic situation is still going on and off, but this won't stop our passion to guide the young generation to achieve their Japan's dream. During this period, we can't say that this is only our teachers put the effort into it, but also all students. We can see that all students put their much and much effort into the study and get this great result. Thank you to all the teachers and students. Well done! You guys did the great job!

You can achieve whatever you want in life. All you have to do is believe that you can. We believe in you, happy graduation~! And good luck with your studies in Japan!
