About Us

WHO ARE WE?

We are an organization that was established in 2008. Our founder and CEO, a native of Japan, Jou Wakabayashi, is very dedicated and passionate about expanding and giving exposure of the language of Japan, Nihongo, and its culture to the world.

OUR GOALS

EduLang's goals are to provide a safe, fun and thriving environment for people to learn Japanese, as well as to ensure that students are able to understand and communicate Japanese effectively in all types of situations.

OUR CENTRE

EduLang Centre is a modern languge learning facility located in Taman Seputeh. Our four-story building houses 15 fully equipped classrooms designed to provide an immersive learning environment. With Japanese-insprired aesthetics both inside and outside the facility, students can expericence a true sense of Japan, enhancing their classroom performance. Vibrant colors like red, pink and white energize students and contribute to the Japanese ambiance.

OUR INSTRUCTORS

Our teachers are native Japanese speakers who possess advanced Japanese language proficieny. They have year of experience in teaching Japanese anf are very passionate about spreading the Japanese culture to people around the world.
